MY SMILE KEEPER_°°°
Litha Kane · May 24, 2018 ·5 minute

All I'm about now in words.
All that should never be done by just saying.
All that she is to me,
Is more than what my heart can afford.
I so long her in my darkest days.
She is my pillar of light.
Obsession is all what I Am
When it concerns her.
I declare in the name of Love;
That my friend I shall love Her,
With all the surroundings of my heart.
Mandithi gxhad'esixhoseni bantakwethu.
Iindlebe zakho lulwazi lwakho,
Ngoko ke uz'undimamele-undima melisisise.
Lee asiyiyo ithetho kawonke-wonke.
Ntombazana ndini!
Andisenako ukuyikhanyela imizwa yam.
Ingqung'entlizweni yam.
Andisenako ukuyikhanyela lemizwa.
Ingquzulana emphefumlweni wam.
Andisenako,
Khawundikwebule thando lwam....


Nicholas L.Kane
