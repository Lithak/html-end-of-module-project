Tarnished Soul I So Long To Keep
Litha Kane · December 16, 2018 ·10 minute

You tarnished my soul ,
vanished the light in me ,
nourished the darkness within me ,
soul drenching ,
and heartbreaking ,
is that really what your good for ?
You literally left me wondering how you could be capable of such ,
how you've perfected the art of lying so much .
You tarnished my soul ,
ripped the bliss off my emotions,
torn apart the essence of my devotions,
chased away the prideful glim off my face.
You figuratively left my emotions to wonder motionlessly
in this dark yet versatile life ,
constantly stabbing me with your words which
penetrate my torso deeper than a knife.
You tarnished My soul ,
Took away my ability to care,
vanquished it till it became rare,
made darkness and me seem like the perfect pair.
You may tarnish me with your spiteful words,
but you'll never decipher who I truly am,
you'll never make me vulnerable to your pathetic ways.

Nicholas L.Kane
